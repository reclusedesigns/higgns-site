Making smart devices smarter.

Higgns automates the world by making smart devices work together.


Smart devices provide a myriad different functions and services in the world. Each device solves a unique set of problems - lights, doors, thermostats... - but they work together in only the most superficial of ways or within closed, proprietary ecosystems. They are the parts in a machine that is missing it’s glue. That is, until now.

Meet Higgns

A system to make the Internet of Things work for you. With Higgns, you can build your own stories and have the smart devices in your environment - across manufacturers - work in concert to deliver the actions you want. When you have smart devices working for you, then you’ll finally experience how much they have to offer.

Higgns is debuting at CES 2015 with Qualcomm and our partners in the AllSeen Alliance. Come see it in action, sign up above to learn more, or - if you are a manufacturer - reach out to us to see how you can have Higgns work with your devices.

Higgns was created by Two Bulls, a boutique product development firm with offices in New York, Melbourne, and Berlin. Two Bulls' mission is to create awesome things that make people's lives suck less.
